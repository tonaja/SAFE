SMART ATTENDANCE AND FACIAL EXPRESSION

Our Smart Attendance and Facial Expression (SAFE) system is designed to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of attendance management by leveraging facial recognition technology. This system can identify individuals' facial expressions to gauge engagement and detect emotional states, ensuring a more interactive and personalized experience.

What solution We Offer You

Inefficiency in Attendance Tracking

Traditional attendance methods are inefficient, as attendance sheets may not reach all students, and some students may mark attendance for their peers. Additionally, students might leave after registering their attendance.

Distractions During Lectures

Various disturbances during lectures, such as phone ring tones, student conversations, and other noises, can significantly distract students and affect their concentration.

Lack of Student Behavior Monitoring

There is a need for a system that not only tracks attendance but also monitors student behavior during lectures to provide feedback for further study and improve the overall quality of education.

Our Services

SAFE utilizes advanced facial recognition technology to streamline attendance tracking and monitor facial expressions. By doing so, it helps organizations maintain accurate attendance records while also providing insights into participants' emotional states, enhancing overall engagement and productivity.

Student

For students, SAFE provides a seamless way to record attendance and track their emotional well-being during classes. The system can help identify students who may need additional support, ensuring a more inclusive and supportive learning environment.

Doctor

For doctors and medical professionals, SAFE offers a reliable method to manage patient appointments and monitor patients' emotional responses during consultations. This can lead to improved patient care and better management of medical records.

Monetar

Monitors can use the SAFE system to oversee attendance and engagement in various settings, from corporate training sessions to public events. The system's facial expression analysis can provide valuable insights into participants' reactions and overall engagement.

Team

Our team is dedicated to developing and maintaining the SAFE system, ensuring it remains at the forefront of facial recognition and emotional analysis technology. We are committed to providing a reliable, user-friendly solution for all your attendance and engagement tracking needs.
